About Us

Headquartered in the Philippines, CS Asia integrates a multi-solution portfolio into a unified strategy for public sector modernization—offering advanced capabilities in cloud seeding, weather radar, mobile command vehicles, aviation weather systems, flood protection, and UAV-based geospatial intelligence. These solutions form a comprehensive “climate-to-command” stack, supporting governments before, during, and after crisis events.

What distinguishes CS Asia is its channel-first distribution model: a single, high-accountability distributor per country, supported by pre-sales engineering, local demo infrastructure, and strict compliance with international procurement and anti-bribery standards. This ensures both market responsiveness and regulatory integrity, especially in B2G engagements.

CS Asia does not operate as a traditional reseller. It acts as a platform orchestrator—localizing globally developed technologies, aligning them with national priorities, and embedding support infrastructure to enable long-term performance. Through pilot deployments, public-private partnerships, and donor-aligned initiatives, the company helps translate innovation into measurable national outcomes.

The company is a strategic affiliate of Critical Solutions Global, with investment and board-level continuity provided by CS Global principals. While independently registered and managed, CS Asia shares in the broader group’s vision and serves as its operational anchor in Southeast and South Asia.

With expansion plans underway for a regional headquarters in Singapore and satellite hubs in India and Indonesia, CS Asia is positioning itself as the leading integrator of high-impact public technologies in the region—building not just projects, but national capabilities.

Robert W. Kamerling
CEO & President

I first traveled to Asia in 1992 and spent six months exploring different countries. From that point forward, I was in love with Asia and always had the idea of one day living in Asia in the back of my mind.

I started working in South East Asia in 2018 and very quickly became awe struck with the Philippines. I quickly realized that I felt at home here in so many ways and developed a deep appreciation for the people, the culture, and the general attitude of acceptance that permeates society here. I decided that I wanted to do something meaningful with my expertise in technology and weather-related security that would benefit the people and the economy of the Philippines. I am very passionate about growing Critical Solutions Asia, Inc. and leaving a lasting legacy in the place I proudly call home.
